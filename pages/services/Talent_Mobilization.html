Our contract staffing solutions provide qualified, job-ready contractors for temporary and temp-to-hire roles across a wide range of job functions and industries. We rapidly source specialized talent to meet your urgent contract needs.

Direct hire Staffing

DivIHN Direct, our direct hire staffing program, transcends typical recruiting. We don’t just fill roles, we build exceptional teams tailored to each client’s unique business goals and culture.

Our consultative approach involves deeply understanding your specific needs and priorities. This enables us to identify professionals who will integrate seamlessly and drive your business forward.

We invest substantial time with both clients and candidates to ensure the right match. Aligning ambitions, abilities and objectives to create a positive impact.

For technology roles, our internal SMEs lend their expertise to informed screening and selection. And, with the help of our colleagues involved with DE&I initiatives we also tap our diverse networks to actively seek diverse candidate slates by leveraging partnerships with affinity groups and diversity-focused organizations at the local and national level.

Our refined process handles every hiring detail - from sourcing great candidates to screening, interviewing, assessments and negotiation. We focus on quality over quantity, and fit over simply filling roles.

Flex-Talent

Today’s IT leaders need flexible access to specialized expertise that can turbocharge projects and enable innovation. But conventional consulting models don’t always fit the bill for part-time, short-term, or interim needs.

That’s why we built FlexForce - to provide on-demand specialized talent through flexible staffing models. Get targeted skills to drive initiatives forward without unnecessary overhead.

With FlexForce, you can supplement your teams with experts in areas like cybersecurity, cloud, analytics, and more. Access niche skill sets or expanded capacity without permanent headcount.

We offer both self-managed and externally-managed flexible talent. Scope talent to the exact duration and level you require. We’ll handle recruiting, onboarding, and offboarding seamlessly.

FlexForce also leverages our communities of specialized experts ready for project-based work. We tap into these networks for proven talent you can deploy rapidly.

FlexForce gives you on-demand access to the skills you need, when you need them.
