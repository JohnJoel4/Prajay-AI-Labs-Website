Enterprise Architecture

We create Holistic Solutions for our clients using Applied Enterprise Architecture principles.

Overview

Adaptability is crucial in enterprise architecture. Technology leaders must be ready for change as requirements evolve. The business technology they manage should enable rapid responses to internal and external shifts. This urgency emphasizes the importance of adaptability in enterprise architecture.

To carry out the work of enterprise architecture, technology leaders must have a seat at the organization’s strategy table and allow for the inevitable changes of plan so that when requirements change. The business technology they manage must be a responsive enabler, not a slow-moving impediment to responding to the internal and external forces of change.

Adaptive Enterprise Architecture and IT Strategy

As an expression of the core purpose of Enterprise Architecture, DivIHN helps customers apply holistic technology concepts to the strategic enablement of business processes. An Adaptive Enterprise Architecture perspective emphasizes flexibility and adaptability, without security compromises, as highly valued criteria for decision-making. We promote holistic Enterprise Architecture thinking that considers more than just technology. Equally important are the people, their processes, and their tools.

Enterprise architecture is often expressed visually through current and future state reference architecture diagrams, the identification of projects required to achieve that future state, and a roadmap to carry them out. In its fullest sense, when it encompasses organizational, governance, and technology planning, Enterprise Architecture is central to an effective IT strategy.

Applications and Solutions Architecture

For Synapse AI, Applications and Solutions Architecture focuses on flexibility and adaptability to support evolving business needs. We design solutions using the most appropriate technologies to solve complex problems with flexible integrations and reusable components. Key considerations include adopting microservices, APIs, and modern design patterns like Low Code/No Code, which provide scalability and enable rapid updates. We also prioritize data integration and analytics, ensuring seamless data flow across systems and leveraging advanced tools like AI and machine learning for actionable insights and data-driven decision-making. This approach ensures businesses can adapt quickly and make informed decisions.

Infrastructure and Cloud Architecture

Synapse AI's Infrastructure and Cloud Expert Architects help customers make strategic technology decisions by designing robust, scalable, and reusable foundations that support business operations. We emphasize cloud computing for scalability and agility, recommending cloud-based solutions to meet evolving needs. Our approach includes hybridization, modularity, and heterogeneous attack surfaces to create secure, cost-effective, and high-performing systems with seamless integration. Additionally, we advocate for software-defined infrastructure, such as software-defined networks and micro-segmentation, to enhance security and scalability within a Zero Trust Architecture.

Security Integrations

This involves creating a cross-disciplinary working group with hybrid roles, guided by leaders like a Chief Technology Officer. This approach fosters decision-making, professional development for technical managers, and contributes to organizational learning, team development, and job enrichment.
